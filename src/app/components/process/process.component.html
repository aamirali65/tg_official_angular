Our Process

01 Select your Package

It is suggested to select your package according to your business needs and wants you can consult us before placing your order to get free consultation for selecting your package.

02 Provide Information

You can share your thought and ideas before start working on your designs. If you have any ideas or concepts to share you are independent to do so. If not then you need to fill out the brief form to get an idea of what type of logo you want for your business.

03 Incept Design Process

Once we have all the information we start working on your design process it takes 12 hours to create initial concepts for your logo.

04 Feedback And Revisions

We present our initial designs to you and get your feedback on them and if any changes need to be made we do it I minutes. We are not done until your final design.

05 Launch And Final Delivery

Once you finalize your design and give us ago-ahead for final file delivery. We begin to create high-resolution vector files to deliver to you in any desired format. Because it is important to deliver the final files in your desired format which will be used for printing purposes in the future.
